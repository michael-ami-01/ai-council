# AI Council — 18 Expert Perspectives on Any Question

Get instant analysis from 18 AI-powered expert personas — from Physicist to Philosopher, Startup Founder to Skeptic. Ask any question and receive a synthesized verdict drawing on diverse perspectives.

🔗 **[Try it live →](https://michael-ami-01.github.io/ai-council/)**

## How It Works

1. Enter any question or topic
2. AI Council consults 18 expert personas in parallel batches
3. Each expert responds from their domain expertise
4. A synthesis engine distills the key insights and verdict

## Expert Personas

Physicist, Biologist, Economist, Historian, Philosopher, Psychologist, Engineer, Legal Scholar, Data Scientist, Startup Founder, Military Strategist, Environmental Scientist, Ethicist, Political Scientist, Neuroscientist, Sociologist, Mathematician, and Skeptic.

## Usage

Bring your own API key (Anthropic Claude or OpenAI). Your key stays in your browser — never sent to any server.
